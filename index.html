Alohomora

Opens locks on doors.

Aparecium

Reveals things written in invisible ink or hidden by a concealing charm.

Wingardium Leviosa

Makes objects levitate.

Arresto Momentum

Makes objects stop moving, or slow in velocity.

Ascendio

Flings the caster into the air.

Descendio

Causes the target object to lower to the ground.

Expelliarmus

Disarms the target witch or wizard.

Finite Incantatem

A counter spell- makes other spells stop.

Incendio

Conjures fire, or lights the target object on fire.

Locomotor

Makes the target object move.

Lumos

Lights the end of the wand.

Meteolojinx

Stops spells and charms affecting the weather.

Nox

Extinguishes the end of the wand.

Reparo

Repares the target object.

Revelio

Removes magical disguises when used on a person, otherwise reveals concealed objects.

Stupefy

Stuns the target of the spell.
